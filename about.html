About

Xeon Protocol was founded in 2023 as Neon Hedge. The protocol was founded out of a real need in March 2023, where we noticed only major alts are supported on all hedging and lending platforms.

The protocol's codebase has gone under continued iterations to and continues to receive updates. The protocol development has been led by   Byte Zero, the team has grown to 2 members as of Januray 2024. Team growth is anticipated as the product moves closer to market release.
